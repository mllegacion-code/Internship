My Role

During my internship in the Finance and Administrative Division, I supported financial data management and administrative processes through data-driven tasks. My tasks primarily focused on organizing, validating, reconciling, and analyzing financial information using Microsoft Excel and government financial systems. The internship emphasized the integration of finance and data analysis, providing practical experience in transforming raw financial records into organized information that supports reporting.
